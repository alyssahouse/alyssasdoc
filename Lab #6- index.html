The Iconic Crab Cooker

The eclectic paintings, hand-carved statues, and refreshing interior design will make you want to come back again and again. History is sprinkled all over the walls at the Crab Cooker and the decor provides a walk down memory lane as you wait for the best clam chowder you'll ever have. Even those who aren't a fan of seafood will crave the unique recipe of ther Manhattan Clam Chowder. The windows are open to bring in fresh air from the ocean breeze and the environment is more than welcoming. Come on down & avoid the crowds to try a new experience at the most unlikely time of year.

Fashion Island

For both nature lovers and those who enjoy shopping as their workout. Fashion Island is home to plenty of popular stores a short drive away from the ocean. This outdoor mall is beautifully designed to keep the charming beach environment alive and will often have sand sculptures in the main courtyard. Fashion Island is a great compromise for those who want to get a special treat for Christmas Eve while enjoying the classic holiday tunes. The weather during this time of year is often very plesant to walk around outdoors and yet another way to partake in any last minute shopping without overwhelming crowds.

Sunset of Your Day

The perfect day starts out with enjoying a fresh, flavorful lunch at the Crab Cooker followed by walking along the glistening shore. Take a walk down the pier to capture amazing views and see the fishers' catch of the day. Wind down by shopping at Fashion Island to check out clothing, books, and more. This itenerary is my personal reccomendation & a special tradition for over 15 years & is sure to provide a lovely day.
